Travel Fun

I-80

Exits 34/34A, 35/35A - Dover, NJ

  • Las Tres Marias - 88 N Sussex St, Dover, NJ

    Fantastic Peruvian restaurant. Not fancy in the slightest, but man can they cook! Everything here is made in house and nothing - except the bottles of water and cans of soda - comes pre-packaged. You want killer local peruvian food? This place right here is where it's at.

    Pan Con Chicharron - Killer fried pork sandwich. Comes on a peruvian roll, chunks of fried pork with nice bits of fat on a bed of fried sweet potato. Tasted amazing. I highly recommend getting the hot sauce. Creamy, bold, and spicy - perfect for fatty pieces of meat like this.

    Lomo Saltado Carne - Strips of beef with onions, tomatoes, soy sauce and french fries served with a heaping plate of rice. Excellent dish, cooked to perfection and delicious.

I-84

Exits 15A/B, 19A/B - Middletown, NY

  • Rock Fantasy - 12 E Main St, Middletown, NY

    A great getaway for fans of rock, metal, and pinball. Yes, pinball. A lot of pinball. Some really cool (and sometimes obscure) movies as well as records, CDs, clothes, posters. Cool place, with cool people running the joint. Stop on by and plunge some quarters into the pinball machines.

  • The Switch Inn - 126 E Main St, Middletown, NY

    Great dive bar. Local place with local vibes where the waitresses and customers all know each other. Not really a sports bar - they have a jukebox and Kino playing in the background. Place smells of alcohol and bartenders friend.

    Philly Cheese Steak - "Tasted like I was back standing outside of Sunny's"

    Jalapeno Ppoppers - JP's have a bad rep of being nothing but microwaved frozen snacks, but the JP's here are pretty good. Nice and crispy, great breading, jalapeno's had a crunch, and even a touch of spice to them. Might be made in-house (but are probably just a really high quality premade product).

    French Onion Soup - Holy crap. A FOS that actually tastes like onions?! And has a boat-load of onions in the bottom of the ramekin? Yes please. Again, I can't tell you if this was a pre-made product or home-made but I can tell you that it tasted great. If you like FOS this is a must have.
